Services

We offer a range of different services such as fireplace installations. This lets users be able to pick which one of our services offer the best solution for them in their personal scenario. We have plenty of different options for the different services we offer and install.

Wood Fireplace

We offer wooden fireplace installments which use real wood to heat up the house. These are the most authentic looks we can offer.

Gas fireplaces

We also offer gas fireplaces which can be installed cheaply, these fireplaces are becoming more popular in recent years. This is due to the ability to be able to have it look realistic and without needing to always buy wood for it.

Outdoor fireplace

We also offer outside fireplace installments which can be nice in the summer and to invite family around and still stay warm throughout the year. These will be wooden fireplaces and can also be moved around.

Outdoor fireplace

Element 4 fireplaces allow luxury features in a house by combining electric and gas fires which can suit any customers requirements. Commonly will be incorporated into a wall.
